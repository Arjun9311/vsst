Our Work

Programmes that transform communities

From residential childcare to women's empowerment, our programmes address the most critical needs of marginalised populations across Telangana.

Children's Home

Safe residential care with structured routines, discipline, and a loving family environment for orphaned and semi-orphaned children aged 4–12.

120+ children in careLearn More

Education Support

Complete educational assistance including school enrolment, uniforms, stationery, tuition, and computer literacy for underserved children.

500+ students supportedLearn More

Women's Development

Empowering women through Self-Help Groups (SHGs), skill training, microfinance access, and community leadership development.

15+ active SHGsLearn More

HIV/AIDS Awareness

Community-level awareness campaigns, testing facilitation, de-stigmatisation drives, and support for affected individuals and families.

5,000+ lives reachedLearn More

Environment

Tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and awareness programmes promoting environmental sustainability in local communities.

Ongoing initiativesLearn More

Care for the Aged

Planned residential homes for elderly men and women without family support, ensuring dignity, medical care, and companionship.

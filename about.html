About Vishwashanthi Shrushti Seva Trust

Transforming lives through care and education

Vishwashanthi Shrushti Seva Trust is a registered Indian charitable trust (Reg. No. 768K IV/2024 / REGD.NO: 2091/2001) operating in Telangana. Since 2001, we have been committed to providing holistic care for orphaned and semi-orphaned children, empowering women through Self-Help Groups, and building healthier communities through awareness programmes.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing home where every child receives quality education, balanced nutrition, healthcare, and the discipline to thrive — regardless of their circumstances. We empower women, support communities impacted by HIV/AIDS, and foster environmental stewardship.

Our Vision

A society where no child is denied opportunity because of poverty, and where marginalised communities — women, the elderly, and those affected by disease — are empowered with the resources, skills, and dignity to lead fulfilling lives.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We treat every child, woman, and elder with dignity, respect, and unconditional love.

Transparency

Every rupee is accounted for. Audited reports are published annually and available for download.

Community

We believe sustainable change comes from empowering communities, not creating dependency.

Accountability

Registered and compliant with all Indian regulatory bodies — 80G, 12A, FCRA, and DPDP Act.

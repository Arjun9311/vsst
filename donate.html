Common Questions About Donating

Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes. Vishwashanthi Shrushti Seva Trust is registered under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. You will receive a valid 80G receipt for every donation, which can be used to claim tax deductions.
Can I donate from outside India?
Yes. We are FCRA registered and can accept donations in foreign currency. Foreign donations are processed through our designated FCRA bank account in compliance with Indian law.
How is my money used?
100% of your donation goes directly to programme expenses — shelter, food, education, and healthcare for children. Administrative costs are funded separately through institutional grants.
Will I get updates on the child I sponsor?
Monthly sponsors receive quarterly progress updates including academic performance and well-being reports. We respect children's privacy and follow strict consent guidelines for any photographs shared.
Can I cancel my recurring donation?
Yes, you can cancel or modify your recurring donation at any time through your donor dashboard or by contacting us directly at +91 80745 89091.
